# Software Research Group (SRG) - PUPQC



## 🏛 About Us

The Software Research Group (SRG) is an internal, student-led research and development organization at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines — Quezon City (PUPQC). SRG cultivates technical skills, supports institutional software needs, and mentors members through supervised, impact-driven projects.

## 🛠 What We Do

- Conduct applied and academic research in software engineering, AI, and systems design
- Build and maintain institutional software (student services, faculty tools, admin systems)
- Provide mentorship, workshops, and professional exposure for members via real-world projects
- Support university extension and digital initiatives in coordination with faculty
- Ensure responsible data handling and adherence to university policies

## 👥 Organizational Chart

- Faculty Adviser
- Executive Lead
- Research Committee
- Software Development Team
- DevOps & Infrastructure Support
